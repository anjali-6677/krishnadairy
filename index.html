Welcome to our Website

Welcome to Shree Krishna Dairy. Established in 1975, we’ve proudly served our community for nearly half a century with high‐quality dairy products. From fresh milk, curd, ghee, mava, and lassi to our wide range of ice creams and cold drinks, each item is crafted with tradition, care, and innovation. We go the extra mile by providing door‐to‐door delivery, ensuring you can enjoy our products in the comfort of your home. Whether you’re looking for a nutritious start to your day or a sweet treat on a hot afternoon, Shree Krishna Dairy promises freshness and convenience with every order.

Legal Compliance

At Shree Krishna Dairy, we strictly ensure compliance with all legal norms, whether for our products or brands in the market. We understand its importance and guarantee its fulfilment.

Food Safety Guarantee

We believe in the philosophy of "Consumer First". As part of our corporate responsibility, we ensure that food safety is our top priority. All our plants have FSSAI licenses, certifying our commitment to food safety and dependability.

Consumer Preference

Shree Krishna Dairy has a strong focus on its consumers. We go the extra mile to provide consistent quality products that cater to their organoleptic preferences. We not only aim to give consumers the best but also understand their needs through robust customer-connect programs.

Customer Satisfaction

Our customer satisfaction comes not only from consistent quality products but also from our customer care services, which are readily available to provide product-related information and address any queries or concerns.

Supplier Assurance

We strive to ensure consistent quality products with conforming purity, made possible through our own Shree Krishna Dairy Distribution Centre, agents, and retailers. We ensure on-time deliveries so that our products are available to consumers 24/7.

Quality at Best Price

Being in the dairy market, we ensure our products are not only the healthiest and tastiest but also affordable for everyone. Quality is not something we strive for; it’s something we guarantee with consistency.
