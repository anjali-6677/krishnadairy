Rampyare Mishra

Director

In 1975, Mr. Rampyare Mishra started Shree Krishna Dairy with a small shop selling only cow milk and curd. He focused on good quality and customer service, which made people trust him. Because of this trust and increasing demand, he added buffalo milk, mava, and lassi to his products within a couple of years. Mr. Mishra's strong leadership and focus on quality made the dairy a well-known and trusted place for dairy products.

Vedprakash Mishra

Director

Mr. Vedprakash Mishra, Mr. Rampyare Mishra's son, has greatly changed Shree Krishna Dairy since 2001. He is known for his hard work and friendly smile. He took the dairy, which started with just cow milk and curd, and added ice cream, cold drinks, and taak. His friendly way of dealing with customers and starting home delivery made customers trust the dairy even more. Mr. Vedprakash Mishra's focus on quality and new ideas keeps Shree Krishna Dairy a loved and trusted name in the area.

Mrs. Sanju Mishra

Finance

Mrs. Sanju Mishra, the wife of Vedprakash Mishra, manages the finances at Shree Krishna Dairy. She introduced a card payment system that allows customers to buy on credit and pay later, making it easier for them. Her hard work and support, alongside her husband, have helped improve and grow the business.
